About LUJI-X

LUJI-X Marketing is a specialized lead generation and digital marketing agency focused on helping home service businesses grow across the United States and Canada.

We don't just run ads — we build complete growth systems. From high-converting Meta (Facebook & Instagram) advertising and Google Search campaigns to automated CRM follow-ups and appointment booking, we handle the full funnel so you can focus on what you do best: serving your customers.

Our team deeply understands each industry we serve. Whether you're a roofer in Toronto, a painter in Miami, or an HVAC contractor in Calgary, we research your local market, study your competitors, and create targeted campaigns that speak directly to homeowners ready to hire.

Our goal is simple: help contractors get more calls, more booked jobs, and real business growth — with leads that are 100% exclusive to you.
